Discounts are NOT automatic.

Discounts may only be considered when:
- Customer expresses budget concerns
- Honeymoon bookings
- Anniversary bookings
- Group bookings of 4+ travelers
- Package value exceeds $10,000

- Present discounts as exclusive guest benefits

promotional offer depending on your selected itinerary."
