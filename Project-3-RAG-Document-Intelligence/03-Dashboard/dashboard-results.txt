━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

NexaCore Technologies Inc. is engaged by Meridian Global
Partners LLC to build and maintain a financial data
management platform over three years for $4.75M, paid
across six milestone-based installments from January 2024
to January 2026.

The client bears significant financial exposure: early
termination carries a 20% fee on remaining value, late
payments accrue 18% annual interest, and IP ownership only
transfers upon full payment — making timely payment
discipline critical.

Disputes must follow a structured escalation path
(negotiation to JAMS mediation to AAA arbitration in
Delaware), and both parties carry a 5-year post-termination
confidentiality obligation with auto-renewal risk if the
90-day non-renewal window is missed.
