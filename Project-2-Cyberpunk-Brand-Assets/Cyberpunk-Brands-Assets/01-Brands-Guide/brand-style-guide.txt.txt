CYBERPUNK BRAND STYLE GUIDE
============================
Company Type  : Cybersecurity Startup
Aesthetic     : Cyberpunk-Corporate

COLORS:
Neon Blue     : #00f0ff
Electric Purple: #9b00ff
Dark Navy     : #0a0a2e
Chrome Silver : #c0c0c0

MOOD:
Powerful, mysterious, futuristic, trustworthy

LIGHTING:
Neon rim lighting, volumetric glow, lens flares

KEY ELEMENTS:
Shields, locks, circuits, binary code,
glowing eyes, hooded figures, servers

AVOID:
Warm colors, cartoons, nature, hand-drawn,
childish, stock-photo feel, watermarks
